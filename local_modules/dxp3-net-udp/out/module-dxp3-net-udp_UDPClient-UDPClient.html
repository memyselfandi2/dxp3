Class: UDPClient

dxp3-net-udp/UDPClient~UDPClient(args)

Use an UDPClient to send UDP messages over the network. It does not listen for incoming messages. It simply fires and forgets. We explicitly call bind() to allow us to specify broadcast and/or multicast settings in case we are using broadcast or multicast addresses.
